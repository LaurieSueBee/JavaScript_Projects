NOTE: indexOf() and search()...Although very similar in appearance and function--
indexOf() and search() ARE NOT EQUAL.


    These are the differences:
  • The search() method canNOT take a second start position argument.
  • The indexOf() method canNOT take powerful search values (regular expressions as parameters/arguments).
